About Sakura Sips

Sakura Sips blends the rich tradition of Japanese tea culture with the bold, expressive style of modern streetwear. Our unique tea sets are designed to bring a fresh, urban edge to your tea drinking experience while honoring the timeless rituals of tea preparation.

FAQ's

What types of tea sets do you offer?

We offer a wide range of Japanese-inspired tea sets, from traditional designs to modern, streetwear-influenced pieces. Our collection includes matcha sets, sencha sets, and unique fusion designs that blend Japanese aesthetics with urban style.
